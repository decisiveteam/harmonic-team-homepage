What It Is

Harmonic Team is (soon to be) open-source software for fast group sensemaking.

Observations and Context

Group conflict can often be prevented or resolved by making a clear distinction between observations and the interpretation of those observations. Getting agreement about known facts first, before attempting to make meaning out of those facts, can go a long way towards helping people understand each other's perspectives.
When individuals share their observations first and then what they find meaningful about those observations second, other people in the group gain both knowledge about what was observed as well as insight into the observer's perceptions and values.
This is especially helpful when people express appreciation for specific actions taken by others in the group. Expressions of appreciation build trust, and trust enables effective coordination.

How It Works

Harmonic Team is based on a 3 step process:
1. Notice something.
2. Share your observation.
3. Connect it to other observations.
